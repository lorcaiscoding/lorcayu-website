The EyeKnow Manor (2024)

Immersive Halloween Activation for Fernet-Branca
animatronics, interactive installations

Set inside a reportedly haunted mansion in New Orleans, The EyeKnow Manor was a one-night-only immersive experience that brought Fernet-Branca's dark mystique to life through theatrical storytelling and animatronic spectacle.

As freelance producer, designer, and fabricator, I co-led the creation of two large-scale animatronic characters and interactive installations, each triggered by audience motion. Beyond hands-on building, I managed procurement, fabrication scheduling, and on-site AV integration within a fast-paced three-week timeline.

All components were modularized for efficient transport and rapid installation. Fusing mechanics, sensor-driven interactivity, and brand narrative, the activation invited guests into a surreal haunted world where Fernet's mythology collided with the eerie magic of the season.
