Chungking Express (2024)

co-developed with Kevin Chen, Rachel Li & Kaustav Sarkar
Unreal Engine 5.3, NYU IDM Ride System

Chungking Express is an immersive amusement ride experience created with Unreal Engine 5.3 and the custom ride machine system developed at NYU's Integrated Design & Media program. Presented as a speculative journey aboard a suspended cable car, the work invites participants to explore an abandoned city suspended between ruin and renewal.

The narrative unfolds through a series of spatial and ideological tensions: Localism vs. Globalism, Obsolescence vs. Progress, Indigenous memory vs. Urban reinvention, Subjective embodiment vs. Technological sublimity. As the cable car glides through fragmented architectural landscapes and atmospheric ruins, the ride becomes a metaphorical passage through conflicting visions of the future.

The project was developed with the support of NYU IDM's ride machine system and mentorship from Todd Bryant, in collaboration with Kevin Chen, Rachel Li, and Kaustav Sarkar.
